Tequila Sunrise

Ingredients

45 ml tequila 90 ml of freshly squeezed orange juice 15 ml grenadine orange slice and maraschino cherry for garnish

Intructions

Squeeze the oranges (they must be the yellow ones) and strain. For 90 cl of orange juice, a medium-size orange should be enough. Pour tequila into a tumbler filled with ice. Add the orange juice, mix gently and then add the grenadine, which will slowly slide down to the bottom, creating the wonderful chromatic effect that gave the name to the cocktail. Garnish with a slice of orange and a maraschino cherry and enjoy a delicious and super refreshing cocktail.

Nutrition

192 calories
